Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

How long does a custom furniture project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and size. Simple pieces like chairs take 1-2 weeks, while complete room sets can take 4-6 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during consultation.

Do you provide home delivery and installation?

Yes, we provide free delivery within the city and professional installation for all our furniture. Our team ensures your furniture is properly set up and positioned.

What materials do you use?

We use premium quality materials including solid wood (oak, teak, mahogany), engineered wood, and high-grade hardware. All materials are sourced from certified suppliers.

Do you offer warranty on your furniture?

Yes, we provide a 5-year warranty on all structural components and 2-year warranty on hardware and finishes. We also offer free maintenance service for the first year.
